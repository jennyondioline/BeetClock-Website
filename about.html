About Us

Alex Smith manages Living Hope Farm, a diversified vegetable farm in Harleysville, PA. In the off season he creates smartphone apps for small farmers like himself through his company BeetWorks. Alex has a background in research, and has published several scientific papers on agricultural ecology. This experience taught him the power of data, and he now works to help farmers keep records and cultivate resilience.

Rachel Smith is a designer, coder, writer and vegetable-lover based in Brooklyn, NY. She was excited to work with her favorite (and only) brother to create the user interface for BeetClock.

Katherine Jameson made illustrations for the BeetClock app and website. You can see more of her work here.
